BulkFBUploader Privacy Policy

We tried to keep things simple here. We don't collect personal data. That is, no data will be send to our server.
You Facebook login id/password will be send over to Facebook for login purpose. This information will be store in your PC's local cache and you don't need to login again next time you run BulkFBUploader. The mechanism is the same as you login Facebook via your browser, eg, Chrome, Edge, etc.
